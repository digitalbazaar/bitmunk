Getting Started

First off you will need to get an account by visiting Bitmunk.com and signing up for a free membership.

Next you will need to find some music. Browse our catalog to find a song or album you'd like to purchase. Click on the PeerBuy button to start downloading from the Bitmunk marketplace. Your Bitmunk Personal Edition (BPE) software will launch and ask you to log in and start the purchase.

Once you've selected your account and chosen how much you are willing to spend the BPE will contact sellers in the marketplace to request parts of the files. Your software may contact hundreds of different people around the world while it tries to find the best deal for you.

Only once your download is complete will your BPE authorize and pay for the file, distributing payment amongst the artist and all of the fans who distributed pieces of the file to you.

About Bitmunk Personal Edition (BPE)

Your Bitmunk Personal Edition software provides a great interface for managing your purchases and sales of content on the Bitmunk marketplace. Find great Independent music you love and purchase it, then distribute it to other fans on the Bitmunk marketplace and earn real money for helping artists distribute their music.

The Offline BPE icon will be displayed in the status bar at the bottom of your browser window when the BPE has not been started. If you click the icon or load a purchase from Bitmunk.com the icon will change to the Online BPE icon and load the BPE interface in a tab in your browser.

About Bitmunk

Bitmunk is a service run by Digital Bazaar to help people on the Internet buy and sell digital content from each other in a safe, and secure manner. The Bitmunk Personal Edition software is a Firefox browser plugin which allows you to buy and sell digital content using Bitmunk's secure peer-to-peer network.

Music, movies, books and television are at the heart of any culture. With the advent of the Internet it became easy to spread this culture to the world between the fans, but no solutions were created to compensate the artists and other culture creators.

Bitmunk's marketplace addresses the problem - we've created technology to allow fans to spread their favorite works in a peer-to-peer manner while still financially rewarding both the content creators and the fans.
